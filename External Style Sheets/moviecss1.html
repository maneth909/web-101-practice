Dune: Part Two

"Dune: Part Two" is a 2024 American epic science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve, serving as the sequel to the 2021 film "Dune." The narrative continues the journey of Paul Atreides (Timothée Chalamet) as he aligns with the Fremen of Arrakis to seek revenge against House Harkonnen and fulfill his prophesied role in the universe.
